How To Order

  1. CHOOSE THE BRANDING METHOD

    2. Let us know the type of product to be logoed and the quantity. Ask our team about cost savings on volume orders.

  2. WHAT DO YOU WANT TO BRAND?

    3. Tell us your preference, if you don’t know we can help! *Note that not all items can be embroidered, there are a number of things that can restrict embroidery on your items such as pockets, zippers, linings, seams, thick or delicate fabrics, etc.

  3. SEND US YOUR GRAPHIC/LOGO

    4. Send us your logo and let us know your brand colours for matching (PMS, HEX, etc.), the size and placement. For printing, please submit an AI or EPS file with the font converted to outlines. If you do not have an AI or EPS file, you can send your artwork as .jpg, .png, .svg, .cdr, .pdf, etc., and we’ll take it from there. For embroidery, submit DST or EMB files. If you don’t have these formats, you can send your artwork and we’ll digitize it for you. *Note that a one-time set-up fee may be charged for conversion to vectors and embroidery files.

  4. DATE OF COMPLETION

    5. To ensure that we fully understand the project timeline, please clarify the deadline and the flexibility so we can deliver the best results. A rush charge may apply to time-sensitive projects

  5. GET AN ESTIMATE

    6. Once we have all the information about your order, we’ll create an estimate to give a rough idea of the price and may be subject to change once we conclude the specifics.

  6. GET A MOCK-UP

    7. Once all the details are finalized, we’ll create a visual so you can get an idea of how the final product will look like. Although we strive to make the digital representations as accurate as possible, the final product may not be the same. We’ll let you know if changes need to be made when production starts.

  7. APPROVAL AND PRODUCTION

    8. If everything looks exactly the way you want it, then please reply to your proof with approval. You will receive an email confirming the production date and a follow-up when ready to ship or pick up.

Services

Embroidery

Decorating method that uses needles and vibrant thread to create simple or intricate designs.

HEAT TRANSFERS

Heat transfer printing is an embellishment method that requires printing a design on special paper and then transferring onto an item using heat and pressure.

DYE SUBLIMATED CRESTS

A dye sublimated crest is a patch that is created using a special type of ink that turns into a gas when heated, and then binds with the fibers of the fabric.

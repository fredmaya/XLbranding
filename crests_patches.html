CRESTS & PATCHES

Patches are an easy and cost-effective way of adding style to clothing and accessories, they are long-lasting and can help the brand stand out. A patch is made up of twill and the design is stitched directly into it and finished with an embroidered border. There are various ways of affixing them to products such as with a pin, sewn on, iron-on, and hook and loop backing.

We have different types of patches to choose from:


EMBROIDERED PATCHES

Made with thick, colorful thread attached to durable fabric. They produce raised images, making them feel more dynamic.

WOVEN PATCHES

Made with thin threads and are suitable for artwork with fine details or small lettering.

PRINTED PATCHES

They have no color limitations and work best for small-detail designs, small text, multiple colors, gradients, and shades.

BADGES

We embroider badges with names, titles, departments, or even logos. They typically come in standard rectangular shape, but if you’re looking for a specific size and shape, contact us to discuss custom options.

Our patches usually come without any backing, but we offer iron-on, sew-on, and loop and hook Velcro backing.

HEAT TRANSFERS

(PRINTING)

Heat transfer printing is the most cost-effective and simple way to create custom-printed products. The artwork is printed onto special paper and mirrored for the design to be pressed onto the fabric right side up. We use different types of heat transfer to accommodate your needs:


STANDARD

It's our most versatile and popular heat transfer; it keeps colors bright and vibrant.

REFLECTIVE

Designed to reflect light, making it ideal for visibility in low-light conditions.

REFLECTIVE TAPE

Reflects light directly to the original light source and enhances visibility of the wearer, providing safety in poor visibility conditions.

LOW MELT

For hard-to-press materials, it avoids heat marks. Ideal for water-resistant products.

Heat transfers typically last up to 50+ wash and dry cycles, or one to two years depending on washing frequency. To prolong their lifespan, wash clothes inside out, use warm or cold water, and hang to air dry or machine dry on a low heat setting. Never directly iron on heat transfer as it could melt or burn.
